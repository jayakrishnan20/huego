Our Products

The company is one of the only foundries who compete with GATIC in making manhole covers for airports. It has supplied materials to Mumbai Intl Airport, Delhi Intl Airport, Bangalore Intl Airport, Hyderabad Intl Airport, Sydney Airport Qantas terminal, Dubai International Airport and Hamad International Airport. The company is also the manufacturer of fully finished Grey Cast Iron Castings for various applications and has a vacuum molding line to make counterweights and we are the first one from India to have this technology and have established a capacity of 2500 tons per month to make the same.

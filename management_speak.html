Management Speak

HUEGO ENGINEERING PVT LTD started in 2020 with 3 Directors.

Started for one stop solution to customers.

Sourcing of iron castings, steel castings, machined parts, forged parts and light & heavy fabricated parts.

Yes to CLP concepts (Critical and Low volume Parts ).

Focusing only customer satisfaction by achieving 100% OTD as well as Quality without compromising

profit to the customer as well as Huego.

Our aim is to implement GO ZERO quality culture.

Long term business travel with customer to make WIN-WIN scenario.

Open Cost Working ( OCW).

Our Goals for the Future are as follows

  • Increase Customer Satisfaction
  • Add Capacities to achieve higher economies of scale
  • Increase Systems and Team Capability
  • Improve Productivity and Manufacturing Excellence
  • Sustain our Kaizen programs
  • Give back to the Community through our CSR initiatives

We thank all our partners who have contributed to our success. Let's grow together! Best regards, The Huego Team.
